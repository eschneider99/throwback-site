About Amycakes

Amycakes opened in August of 2009 in downtown Springfield, moving to the Walnut Street location in August of 2014. Specializing in made-from-scratch cakes and cupcakes, pies, cookies, cake truffles and cinnamon rolls, Amycakes is a locally-owned, family business. Everything is made-from-scratch so it tastes as moist, flavorful and delicious as it looks!
